Taskr Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

BMA Nexus

This Privacy Policy explains how Taskr ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by BMA Nexus, collects, uses and protects your information when you use the Taskr mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Taskr collects only the minimum information necessary to provide its service:

  • Email address — only if you choose to create an account for cloud sync. We use this solely to authenticate you via Supabase.
  • Task data — your lists, tasks, settings and preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to our secure cloud database.
  • No tracking, no analytics — we do not collect usage statistics, device identifiers, advertising IDs or any behavioral data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your task data across your devices (cloud sync feature).
  • To send local notifications if you have enabled reminders (these are processed entirely on your device — we never receive or store notification content).
  • We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

  • Local storage — all task data is first stored on your device using secure on-device storage (Capacitor Preferences).
  • Cloud storage — if you sign in, your data is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant and your data is protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own data.
  • Your cloud data is stored in the EU region (Supabase Frankfurt).

4. Account & Deletion

  • Creating an account is entirely optional. The app works fully offline without an account.
  • You can delete all your local data at any time via Settings → Clear all data.
  • To delete your account and all cloud data, send an email to feedback@taskr.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 days.

5. Third-Party Services

Taskr uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (Privacy Policy) — authentication and cloud database.

No advertising networks, analytics platforms or social media SDKs are included in this app.

6. Children's Privacy

Taskr is rated for everyone (3+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
